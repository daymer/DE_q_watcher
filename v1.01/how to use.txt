How to use DE_q_watcher v 1.01:

Create a shortcut for DE_q_watcher.exe with following attributes:

%path to file%\DE_q_watcher.exe -timeout 5000 -usb_serial PBAB5 -time_to_react 30

where:

-timeout: how often to rescan queue
-usb_serial: nuff said. S. serial number of your device
-time_to_react: alarm will launched in x min till expiration

Enjoy!

See https://github.com/daymer/DE_q_watcher for new versions!
